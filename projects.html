Projects

Current

  • Anvay – A topic modeling and visualisation dashboard for Bengali texts. Allows researchers to explore latent themes, visualise word associations, and examine topic-document relationships.
  • Indian Fountain Pen Project – A digital archive and public memory initiative documenting the history, design, and significance of Indian fountain pens through interviews, object studies, and reflections.

Earlier Projects & Collaborations

  • Bichitra – The world's largest digital variorum of Rabindranath Tagore's works. Vinayak contributed to Indic text processing and variant collation design.
  • Letters of 1916 – Ireland's first crowdsourced digital humanities project. Vinayak led visualisation and transcription interface development.
  • Humanities at Scale – A European DH infrastructure initiative. Contributed to multilingual metadata workflows and open pedagogy design.
  • Mount Street Bridge – A public history and 3D simulation project reconstructing the 1916 Easter Rising. Vinayak co-developed its narrative mapping structure and user interface.
  • Recalling Jewish Calcutta – A now-archived memory project documenting the Jewish community in Kolkata through oral histories, photographs, and architectural traces.

Code Experiments

  • Letters1916 Mobile App – A prototype mobile access tool for the Letters of 1916 archive. Built using PhoneGap and Java as an early experiment in mobile-access digital curation.
  • BMSB Guide – An interactive web application narrating the Battle of Mount Street Bridge using LeafletJS and spatial overlays. A visually rich experiment in narrative cartography and historical memory.
