1. Introduction

I teach digital humanities at Shiv Nadar Institution of Eminence. My work is mostly about archives, photographs, and computational methods for reading texts. It does have a habit of wandering, though.

This site is where I keep things. Projects, essays, talks, and a pad of shorter pieces I write when I'm not sure where else to put them. Some of it is finished. A lot of it isn't.

If something here is useful to you, good. If it just gives you a sense of what I'm thinking about, that's fine too.
